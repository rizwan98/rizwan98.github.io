Computer Networks & Operating Systems

The unit provides an introduction to the operation of computer networks operating systems theory and practice.The topics inclue:

  • network components – repeaters, hubs, switches, routers, gateways; protocol stacks – OSI TCP/IP, basic network performance characteristics.
  • Process management: processes and threads, performance benefits of multiprogramming, scheduling algorithms, two-level schedulers.
